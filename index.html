Crystal Collector

Instructions

    A random number will be generated at the start of the game.

    By clicking on one of the four crystals below, a random amount will be added to your score.

    To win, your score must match the random number generated at the start of the game. If your number goes above the random number, you lose.

    The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

    Each time the game starts, the values of the four crystals will change.
